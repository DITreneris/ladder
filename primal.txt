# Corporate Ladder

> **Historical concept v0.1.** Current product spec: [docs/mvp-scope.md](docs/mvp-scope.md) and [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md) § Shipped baseline. UI uses Energy/Deadline; this file retains original Burnout naming for narrative context.

## MVP Concept v0.1

### One-Line Pitch

A fast-paced Telegram Mini App where players climb the corporate ladder while avoiding meetings, reorganizations, and burnout.

---

# Core Idea

Most mobile games simulate fantasy worlds.

Corporate Ladder simulates modern work life.

Players start as interns and attempt to climb to CEO before corporate chaos ends their career.

The game combines:

* Simple Lumberjack-style gameplay
* Workplace satire
* Competitive leaderboards
* Shareable career stories

---

# Core Gameplay Loop

1. Start as Intern
2. Climb the ladder by tapping left or right
3. Avoid corporate obstacles
4. Earn promotions
5. Increase score
6. Fail through burnout, meetings, or reorganization
7. Share results
8. Play again

Target session:

30–90 seconds

---

# MVP Mechanics

## Controls

* Tap Left
* Tap Right

Each tap climbs one ladder rung.

---

## Obstacles

Three obstacle types only:

### Meeting

Blocks one side.

### Reorganization

Forces quick decision.

### Burnout

Reduces available time.

---

## Timer

Constant pressure.

Promotions add time.

Higher levels increase difficulty.

# Humor System

The humor is the product.

Every promotion and failure generates satirical messages.

Examples:

"Promoted to Manager. Calendar destroyed."

"Reached CEO. Budget denied."

"Attended 214 meetings. No decisions made."

"Burned out while updating spreadsheets."

The differentiator is not the mechanic.

Corporate Ladder is essentially:

"Lumberjack meets modern office life."

Easy to understand.
